Millets

Ragi

Ragi, or finger millet, is a nutritional powerhouse rich in calcium, iron, and fiber. Its low glycemic index helps regulate blood sugar levels, making it suitable for diabetic individuals. With its gluten-free nature and versatility in cooking, ragi is a valuable addition to diverse diets. Regular consumption of ragi promotes bone health, aids digestion, and supports weight management efforts.

Price: $3.99 per kg

Korralu

Korralu, also known as foxtail millet, is highly nutritious and gluten-free, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions. Packed with essential nutrients like protein, fiber, and minerals, korralu supports overall health and well-being. Its versatility in cooking allows for the preparation of various dishes, from porridge to savory meals. Regular consumption of korralu may help regulate blood sugar levels and promote digestive health

Price: $4.49 per kg

Barri

Barri millet, or proso millet, is a nutritious grain rich in protein and fiber, supporting overall health. Its versatility makes it ideal for diverse culinary creations, from savory pilafs to wholesome porridges. Being gluten-free, it accommodates dietary restrictions, while its drought-tolerant nature promotes sustainable farming practices. Incorporating barri millet into diets offers a nutritious and environmentally conscious food choice.

Price: $4.99 per kg

Varagu

Varagu, known as kodo millet, stands as a versatile and nutritious grain commonly found in culinary applications. This millet can be transformed into a creamy breakfast porridge, providing a wholesome start to the day, or utilized as a rice alternative in various savory dishes, enriching meals with its distinct texture and flavor.

Price: $5.99 per kg
